Welcome to PDG Commerce


PDG Software would like to thank you for evaluating PDG Commerce, PDG's powerful database enabled e-commerce platform. The purpose of this introduction is to jump start you on your way to configuring PDG Commerce for your Web store. Of course, we are here to help you along the way, if needed. Visit the links area in the menu on the left to access video documentation for PDG Commerce, PDG's FAQ, PDG's Message Board, and the PDG Support forum.

The steps that this introduction will guide you through are accessing PDG Commerce's administration panel, taking a test drive of the software with default configurations, basic integration instructions, and resources that you can utilize to further optimize your use of PDG Commerce.
